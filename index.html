Experience

More than a stay

Whether you’re in the mountains or by the ocean, we blend the ease of a boutique hotel with the comfort of a private residence: thoughtful planning, warm interiors, and trusted partners.

Every reservation begins with a conversation. We want to understand how you travel — kids, grandparents, friends, off-mountain or off-beach days — and then build the stay around that.

Across destinations, a consistent thread runs through each home: generous space, considered design, and the sense that everything you need is exactly where you expect it.

  • ✓ Pre-arrival planning: groceries, crib / high chair, rentals, restaurant suggestions.
  • ✓ Local partnerships for private chefs, massage, transportation, and on-the-ground support.

  • ✓ Direct contact with ownership for clarity, flexibility, and discretion.

About & Contact

A growing, owner-led portfolio

Alta Vista began with one family’s love for time together in inspiring places. We are building a small portfolio of homes in destinations we personally know and love — starting in Park City and thoughtfully expanding into select coastal markets.

Owner-led hospitality

Alta Vista Residences is an owner-operated collection created by a family who spends meaningful time in the mountains and by the ocean. We build the portfolio around the places and homes we would choose for our own trips — then open those doors to guests who value similar things.

Renovations, furnishings, and service are selected with long-term quality in mind. As we add homes in Deer Valley East, Hawaii, Florida, and future destinations, that standard remains the filter for every decision.
